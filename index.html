Our Beginning

Curious souls, we sought to grow,
signed up for the same course, and our conversation flowed.
You wavered on flights, unsure if you'd stay,
while advice from others pulled both ways.
Some said go, and some said stay,
but I asked what would brighten your way.
With all I'd read, I could clearly see—
only you knew what was right to be.
In that moment, it came to you;
you found the answer, clear and true.

Your Gentleness

In quiet moments, your warmth does shine,
A gentle spirit, a heart so kind.
With every word, a soothing balm,
In your presence, the world feels calm.
Through trials faced, your patience flows,
In acts of love, your kindness grows.
I cherish the light that you impart,
For gentle souls like you warm the heart.

Gifts Traveling Halfway Around the World 1

The silence of my room roared loud,
filled with cars, children, and booming loneliness—
but not with you.

The air was thick with scents—
leaves, rain, and my bleeding heart—
but not with yours.

The walls pressed hard, unyielding,
tables and my bones when I hugged myself,
but without your touch.

Then came a package: a radio, a Shiba carrying your perfume,
paintings and photos alive with your presence.

And suddenly, I could hear you,
smell you, feel you—
and knew you were with me.
